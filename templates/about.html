About Us

Welcome to MediAIKTC, where health meets technology for a brighter, healthier future. Our platform empowers individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to take control of their health, making healthcare information accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

We envision a world where access to healthcare information is not just a luxury, but a fundamental right. We aim to bridge the gap between technology and health, providing people with insights into their well-being using cutting-edge tools.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team led by Nabil Ahmed, a second-year CSE (AIML) student. With a keen interest in artificial intelligence and healthcare, Nabil recently won the internal round of Smart India Hackathon (SIH), showcasing innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Our Mission

At MediAIKTC, our mission is to provide a seamless and intuitive platform that leverages the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning. We want to assist you in identifying potential health concerns based on your reported symptoms, while offering a wealth of educational resources to enhance your health literacy.

How We Do It

Our platform uses a robust machine learning model trained on a comprehensive dataset of symptoms and diseases. By inputting your symptoms, our system generates accurate predictions about potential illnesses, allowing you to make informed decisions about your health.

Your Well-being, Our Priority

Your health is our top priority. We understand that navigating healthcare can be complex, so we provide not only accurate predictions but also detailed information about each condition, including recommended precautions, medications, dietary advice, and workout tips.

Join Us on This Journey

We invite you to explore our platform, engage with our educational content, and take control of your health journey. Together, we can revolutionize how individuals access and understand healthcare information.
